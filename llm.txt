# FineData.et - NFC Digital Business Cards Ethiopia

## Overview
FineData provides premium NFC (Near Field Communication) digital business cards in Ethiopia. These cards allow professionals and companies to share contact information, social media profiles, and portfolios via a single tap on a smartphone.

## Pricing Models

### 1. Individual / Personal Card
- **Target:** Freelancers, professionals, single users.
- **Price:** 1,200 ETB per card.
- **Features:** Premium matte finish, instant profile setup, lifetime digital profile, free delivery in Addis Ababa.

### 2. Bulk / Corporate Orders
- **Target:** Teams, companies, and organizations.
- **Pricing Tiers:**
  - 5-19 cards: 1,100 ETB per unit
  - 20-49 cards: 1,000 ETB per unit
  - 50+ cards: 900 ETB per unit
- **Features:** Custom company branding/logo printing, centralized team management dashboard.

## How It Works
1. **Order:** Customers contact via WhatsApp or Telegram to choose card style.
2. **Setup:** The digital profile is linked to the NFC hardware.
3. **Tap:** Information is shared instantly with any NFC-enabled smartphone (No app required).

## Technical Details
- **Technology:** Passive NFC (ISO 14443A).
- **Compatibility:** Works with iOS and Android devices.
- **Sustainability:** Reduces paper waste by replacing thousands of traditional business cards.
