Frequently Asked Questions

Does the other person need an app?
No. The recipient does not need any app or special software. When you tap your card to their phone, your digital profile opens automatically in their default web browser.
Is there a monthly subscription?
No. Our cards are a one-time purchase. You can update your information as many times as you like for free through our platform.
Can I put my own logo on the card?
Yes. We provide full customization services for both individual and corporate orders. Simply send us your high-resolution logo via Telegram after placing your order.
Do you deliver outside of Addis Ababa?
Yes, we deliver nationwide. While delivery inside Addis Ababa is typically completed within 24-48 hours, orders to other regions may take 3-5 business days via reliable courier services.
