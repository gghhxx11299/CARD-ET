F.A.Q.

How does the card work?

Simply tap the card against any smartphone. It uses NFC (Near Field Communication) to transmit your digital profile link instantly without needing any special app.

Is it compatible with my phone?

All iPhones from 2018 onwards and almost all modern Android phones have NFC capabilities built-in and enabled by default.
